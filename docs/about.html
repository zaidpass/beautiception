Hi, I'm Urooj

Makeup artist. Content creator. Beauty educator. Mom. Usually all four before noon.

I'm Urooj — a freelance makeup artist, content creator and beauty educator, and an all-encompassing beauty enthusiast. I've been fortunate enough to travel to over 30 countries, learning about different beauty standards and gaining unique insights that have helped me elevate my craft.

For makeup clients, my top priority is your skin health and satisfaction. I only use top-of-the-line, gentle products in my kit — each one carefully selected for quality, performance, durability and its ability to cater to a variety of skin types and tones. Your makeup won't just look stunning; it will feel comfortable and healthy on your skin.

For brands, I specialize in user-generated content, still photography, videography and product styling — content that resonates with your audience and aligns with your brand's image and values.
